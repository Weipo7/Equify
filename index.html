    About Us

    Diversity is arguably one of the largest problems in the technological industry. Equify aims to resolve this issue by providing high school students of all racial and gender groups with a technologically developed opportunity document customized to their needs.

    Our Mission

    We hope to give those who have been marginalized, an opportunity that they can seize and capitalize on. We believe, everybody should have an equal chance to experience STEM and to decide whether or not they want to be a part of it.

    Our Plan

    Equify hopes to provide students who have been robbed of their chance in STEM with tech opportunities. We aim to accomplish this task through a technologically developed form that allows students to customize the document based on their interests.

    Our Vision

    Through our technologically developed opportunity documents, we hope to create an environment that enables young learners all over the globe to immerse themselves in STEM and give them a taste of what the world has in store for them.

    Services

    Upon completing the Equify Technology Opportunity form a document will be sent in approximately 10-15 minutes. The Equaify opportunity document is broken down into 6 components.

    Internships

    Potential internships you can apply to. Internships are strongly related to your field of interest.


    Courses

    High-quality and credible introductory courses/services for your field of interest and grade.

    Hackathons

    Future online hackathons you can participate in to both apply your new skills and to build connections.

    Connections

    Professionals you can connect with through email to help build your knowledge on your field of study.


    External Programs

    Cooperations that offer more resources to help you achieve your full potential on your journey to joining the workforce.

    More Resources

    General resources and advice to help guide you on your successful path of becoming an engineer.

    Code Base

    The backend of the application consisted of web scraping and pdf creation/deployment. Equify, made use of the python BS4, PyPDF3 and STPLIB library to accomplish backend duties. For the front end basic HTML, CSS, and javascript were used. For the form, Equify used the 123 form builder framework along with PHP.
